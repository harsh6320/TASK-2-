TAKE SIP FROM BUBBLE TEA

Have a cup of tea made from bubble tea and add flavour to your day. Bubble tea originated in the city of T'ai-nan, Taiwan, in the mid-1980s. Dozens of variations of the drink have evolved since then, especially throughout Taiwan and other East Asian locales such as Singapore, Hong Kong, and Japan. Popular renditions include taro tea, brown sugar tea, and matcha green tea.
